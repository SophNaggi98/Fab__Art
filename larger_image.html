Information

Material: 18 ct gold chain (carat) and 22 ct. sphere with 0.2 ct. diamond..

Size: Chain 45 cm long. Sphere diameter 1 cm.

Description: The chain consists of small rings, which are then formed and twisted into single knits, to then be beaten into a quadrangular section, which is intended to result uniform and soft.

The sphere is made from two soldered hemispheres, made in sheet form and then hammered with punches. With bezel for the brilliant and ring for the chain.
