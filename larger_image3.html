Information

Material: 18 ct gold (carat). In the centre a brilliant 0.22 ct.

Size: Diameter 2 cm.

Description:
The flower is made by soldering two suitably shaped cups, one inside the other with the bezel for the brilliant in the centre, and then once soldered, worked until the softness and movement of the petals is achieved.
The small rose was then soldered onto two bands forming the ring, giving a sense of lightness and transparency.
