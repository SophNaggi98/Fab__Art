Information

Material: Made of life-size wax then casted in bronze.

Size: Three metres high.

Description: The monument named ‘In flight’ is located in the municipality of Canale Monterano-Rm and is dedicated to the symbol of the municipality's nature park, the Short-toed Eagle, the largest diurnal bird from the area.
